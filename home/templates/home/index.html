How to? Book Market



1. Fill out the information of the book you want to register, determine the deadline, initial price and immediate purchase price. Then, Register.

2. Once the bidding period is over, or if someone chooses to buy immediately so the buyer is determined, you'll know the buyer's information.

3. If you have a book you want to buy, bid or buy immediately. Once you become a buyer, you will know the seller's information.

4. From My Page, you can check or modify information about the auction items you bid or registered.


※ moaU's Book market guarantees anonymity. Mutual information is available on My Page.

How to? Group Buying
